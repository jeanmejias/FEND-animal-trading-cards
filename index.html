Hawk

Type: Air

Hawks are known for their courtship dance where male and female rise to a certain height and then they free-fall together.

  • Size: varies across species; mostly small
  • Age: between 10 and 20 years in the wild
  • Speed: very fast
  • Meals: small mammals, insects, smaller birds

Hawk is a general term for a large group of birds of prey of the order Falconiformes. During daytime, they are swift hunters that strike down on small mammals, insects, or even other birds. They are popularly known for their courtship dance.
